Unrequited Embers

In the realm of heartfelt tales, let me share

A poem born from longing, a tale of love's affair

For in the chambers of my soul, a fragile heart does dwell

A story of rejection, a tale I must now tell.

"Unrequited Embers," the title declares

A bittersweet symphony of unspoken prayers

Where love's flame flickered, but failed to ignite

Leaving me entangled in the depths of night.

Once, I dared to dream of a love so pure and true

With hope in my eyes, I reached out for you

Like a wayward star, you captured my gaze

A beacon of light, guiding me through life's maze.

But alas, my love, it seems fate had its way

For the embers of affection were not meant to stay

In the shadows of uncertainty, my heart grew weak

As I braced myself for the words I longed not to speak.

"Unrequited Embers," a painful refrain

Echoing through the chambers of my soul's domain

With trembling voice and bated breath, I poured my heart

Yet your eyes spoke of a truth that tore me apart.

The words that followed cut through like a knife

And the bitter taste of rejection clouded my life

But in the depths of despair, I found strength anew

For love's rejection is but a chapter, not the whole view.

For I am more than unrequited affection's sting

I'll rise from the ashes, spread my broken wings

I'll find solace in the whispers of the gentle breeze

And seek solace in the moon's silver rays that appease.

"Unrequited Embers," a testament to endure

A tale of resilience, of a heart that remains pure

Though scars may linger, love's flame will still ignite

In the darkest nights, stars reclaim their light.

Cherishing the memories, the moments we once shared

"Unrequited Embers" cannot define how much I've dared

So, I'll stand tall, embracing life's winding road

For love's rejection is but a moment, a tale bestowed.

In the tapestry of life, this chapter will unfold

And in the beauty of resilience, a new story will be told

"Unrequited Embers" shall illuminate my way

As I walk with grace, into a brighter day.

For love's rejection is just a stumble, not my final fall

With steadfast hope, I'll rise above it all

"Unrequited Embers" shall flicker, then fade away

As I embrace the love that awaits another day.
